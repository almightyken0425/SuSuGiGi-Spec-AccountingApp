$wish Import Field Guide

transaction_datetime
  Required
  Date and time, YYYY-MM-DD or YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS.
  Date-only defaults the time to 00:00:00.

category
  Required
  Category name.
  Income or expense follows the amount sign.

account
  Required
  Account name.
  Matched together with its currency.

amount
  Required
  A positive value is income, a negative value is expense.
  Use a period for decimals, up to 4 places.
  No comma, thousands separator, or currency symbol.

currency
  Required
  ISO 4217 code, e.g. TWD, USD, JPY.
  Case-insensitive.

note
  Optional
  Free-text memo.

Notes

- A row is skipped if a required field is empty, or if its date, amount, or currency is not in an accepted format.
- A positive amount is recorded as income, a negative amount as expense.
- Accounts are matched by name and currency. The same name with a different currency creates a separate account.
- If a field value contains a comma, wrap it in double quotes, e.g. "lunch, drinks".
- Leading and trailing spaces in names are ignored.
- Timezone: you may add an offset suffix to a datetime, such as +08:00 or -05:00, or Z for UTC.
  Example: 2026-01-21 12:30:00+08:00
- When a datetime carries its own offset, the source timezone selected on the import screen does not apply to that row.
